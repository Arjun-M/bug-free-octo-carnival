# CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md - Community Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, the contributors and maintainers of IsoBox, pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Helping new contributors get started
- Acknowledging mistakes and learning from them
- Respecting boundaries and consent

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Insults, derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or spamming
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Threats or violence
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behaviors

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub, Discord, mailing lists, etc.)
- Official project events or conferences
- When representing the project in public spaces

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying standards and enforcing them fairly.

### Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Direct Communication**: If safe and comfortable, address the person directly
2. **Report to Maintainers**: Email arjun@builderengine.space with:
   - Description of the incident
   - People involved
   - When/where it occurred
   - Any documentation (screenshots, etc.)
   - Preferred outcome
3. **Confidentiality**: Reports are kept confidential
4. **Response**: Maintainers will respond within 7 days

### Investigation & Response

Maintainers will:
1. Review reported incidents carefully
2. Gather additional context if needed
3. Consult with involved parties (if appropriate)
4. Take appropriate action, which may include:
   - Warnings or reminders of conduct standards
   - Temporary or permanent bans
   - Removal of content
   - Escalation to platform administrators

## Consequences

Consequences for unacceptable behavior may include:

- **First Offense**: Warning and expectation of behavior change
- **Repeated Offense**: Temporary suspension or ban from community spaces
- **Severe Offense**: Immediate permanent ban

The severity and consequences are at the discretion of community leaders.

## Appeal Process

If you believe action taken against you was unjust:

1. Send appeal to arjun@builderengine.space within 30 days
2. Include:
   - Your perspective on the incident
   - Why you believe the action was unjust
   - What you will do differently

3. A different maintainer will review your appeal
4. Decision will be made within 14 days
5. Appeal decision is final

## Guidelines for Positive Interactions

### For Contributors

- Assume good intent
- Be patient with newcomers
- Share knowledge generously
- Provide constructive feedback
- Acknowledge contributions

### For Maintainers

- Be transparent about decisions
- Respond promptly to issues
- Explain reasoning clearly
- Be consistent in enforcement
- Advocate for the community

### For Everyone

- Use respectful language
- Listen actively
- Be open to different perspectives
- Give others benefit of the doubt
- Stand up against violations you witness

## FAQ

**Q: Is this code mandatory?**
A: Yes. By participating in this community, you agree to follow it.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**
A: We believe in growth. Good-faith mistakes are opportunities to learn.

**Q: Can I call out violations?**
A: Yes, but report to maintainers rather than escalating publicly when possible.

**Q: What if the person accused is a maintainer?**
A: Report to the project lead. An uninvolved person will handle the investigation.

**Q: How long are bans?**
A: Temporary bans are typically 30-90 days. Permanent bans are for severe/repeated violations.

**Q: Can I be part of enforcement?**
A: Yes! We welcome community members to help maintain our inclusive space.
